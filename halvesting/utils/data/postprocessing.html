Postprocessing¶

class halvesting.utils.data.postprocessing.Postprocessing(text: str, lang: str, **kwargs)[source]¶

Class used to postprocess text data. Contains functions to clean the texts, normalize them before running some statistics on them.

Notes

Most of the functions used here come from the togethercomputer’s implementation computed when building RedPajama [1] . The original functions where introduced by Raffel et al [2] , Penedo et al [3] and Rae et al [4] .

Warning

Most of the methods involving computing the number of words of a text won’t work as expected on Chinese and Japanese text as they often don’t use blank spaces to separate “words”.

Parameters:

  • text (str) – The raw text to be processed.

  • lang (str) – ISO-636 language code of the text retrieved from HAL.

HuggingFace tokenizer used to tokenize the raw content. By default, the tokenizer used is “google/mt5-base”. The slow tokenizer from HF is the preferred one as the fast one can hang for a while on long sequences .

rps_doc_unigram_entropy()[source]¶

Calculates the entropy of the unigram distribution of the content.

Notes

This measures the diversity of the content and is computed using

\[\sum_{S}(\frac{-\vert w\vert}{\vert S \vert} * log(\frac{\vert w \vert}{\vert S \vert}))\]

where the sum is taken over over counts of unique words in the noramlized (punctuation removed, lowercased) content \(S\).

Returns:

score – The entropy of the unigram distribution of content.

Return type:

float

rps_lines_frac_start_with_bulletpoint()[source]¶

Calculates the ratio of lines that start with a bullet point symbol.

The following set of unicodes are considered a bullet point: u2022 (bullet point), u2023 (triangular bullet point), u25B6 (black right pointing triangle), u25C0 (black left pointing triangle), u25E6 (white bullet point), u25A0 (black square), u25A1 (white square), u25AA (black small square), u25AB (white small square), u2013 (en dash).

Returns:

score – The ratio of lines that start with a bullet point symbol.
